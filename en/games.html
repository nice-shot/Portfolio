Ultimate Election

A one button game a friend and I made for the upcoming elections. In the game you choose a party name (doesn't have to be a real party) and you start voting for it. Votes from all the players are updated in real-time and it becomes a competition to see who can win the most votes before the election date.

Generation Catch

A game I've worked on as a graphic designer in the Global Game Jam competition. An international game development competition where we had to make a game in two days. This game works using a web cam. One player stands in front of the camera trying to hit targets while another player sees a video of him and chooses where to place the targets.
